Visualizing Housing Market Trends with Tableau

The goal is to address challenges in understanding the factors that influence house prices and sales trends.

By analyzing comprehensive housing data—including renovation years, bedroom counts, and floor details—the project uncovers key insights.

Using Tableau dashboards, we visualize patterns that inform strategic decisions, optimize pricing, and improve market competitiveness.
